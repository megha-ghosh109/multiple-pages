About me

Sowtfare developer from West Bengal, India

Hello there! I'm a passionate software engineer currently pursuing my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering in the vibrant state of West Bengal, India. As I delve into the world of technology and coding, I find myself constantly intrigued by the ever-evolving landscape of software development.

Beyond the lines of code, I have a soft spot for animals, and my love for pets knows no bounds. In fact, I proudly identify as an aquarist, finding joy in creating and maintaining beautiful aquatic ecosystems. There's something therapeutic about watching the mesmerizing dance of colorful fish in a well-designed aquarium.

In the midst of challenging programming projects and academic pursuits, I find solace in the company of my aquatic friends and the unconditional love of my pets. It's this balance between the digital and natural worlds that keeps me inspired and motivated on my journey as a software engineer and aspiring aquarist.

Originally hailing from West Bengal, India. The prospect of immersing myself in the vibrant tech community of Bangalore is both thrilling and promising. Feel free to connect if you share a love for technology, aquatic life, or simply want to exchange stories about our furry and finned companions!
